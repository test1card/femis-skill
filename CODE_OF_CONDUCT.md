# Code of Conduct

This project is for careful engineering work. Discussions should stay factual, specific, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be precise about evidence, versions, units, and solver context.
- Criticize claims, recipes, and assumptions directly; do not attack people.
- Mark uncertainty clearly, especially around safety-critical or sign-off-adjacent guidance.
- Keep proprietary model data, credentials, license-server details, and private paths out of public issues and PRs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information or proprietary data.
- Presenting unverified recipes as author-verified results.
- Pressuring contributors to approve unsafe engineering claims or sign-off language.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or serious violations may
lead to a block from the project.

If you need to report conduct privately, contact the repository owner through GitHub.
